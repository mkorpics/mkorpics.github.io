Virtual Pet

Let There Be Dragons

Task: Constructed a console application for an interactive game-like virtual pet program.

Skills: C#, OOP

Loosely inspired by How to Train Your Dragon, my program tells the story of a person who goes to adopt a dragon. Once they adopt it, they can choose different menu options to interact with their dragon. The stats are interconnected, with each option altering both the directly correlated stat and the others that correspond.

Different story scenarios with alternate menus are set to run at regular intervals. Random number generators allow the dragon some degree of autonomy and, in the alternate story lines, send users to different outcomes based on the same choice. If the dragon's stats go too far over or under the optimal range, alternate stories arise as a consequence, based upon which stat was neglected.
